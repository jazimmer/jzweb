Why Study Math or Programming?

Why study mathematics or computer science if you are going to be, say, a movie director?

Because a movie involves creating lots of pieces that must fit together: script, set, cinematography, costuming, acting abilities, etc.

Or suppose you want to be a physician, then why?

Because a physician must plan the activities of nurses and medical technicians. The work of these helpers must be well coordinated with the plan of diagnosis or treatment. A medical practice is more routine than making a movie. Even so the need to do things differently does arise and when routines are changed the need for them to work correctly the first time is greater than on a movie set.

Practicing creative mathematics or computer programming can improve your ability to make complicated plans that just work.
