Five Truths

More than a decade after I wrote Six Daily Goals, I sought five assertions about life that that might be useful in the pursuit of happiness:
  • Life is change, stability comes only with death.
  • Without self knowledge, happiness is fragile.
  • Unhappiness feeds on inflexible desires.
  • The future never comes, something else does.
  • The past never was, you made most of it up.
